STEP

1

Prepare your iPhone and watch. (This tutorial use iPhone speakers)

STEP

2

Refer to the manual of the watch to check whether the "Home City Setting" is correct. Taking "New York" as an example, the watch should select "NYC".

STEP

3

Select "WWVB" in WatchWave, click "START", turn the volume to the maximum, and the iPhone speaker will make a "Da Da Da Da ... " sound.

STEP

4

Refer to the manual of the watch, set the watch to "Perform manual receive" mode, and the watch will display "RC!".

STEP

5

Hold the watch close to the iPhone speaker and wait for the watch to receive radio waves.

STEP

6

After about 6 minutes, the reception is completed and the watch displays "GET".

STEP

7

End wave, check watch time.

*If it fails, please make multiple attempts or use headset, you can also contact us in "Settings".


*If your manual is missing, please contact us in "Settings", we provide free search service.
