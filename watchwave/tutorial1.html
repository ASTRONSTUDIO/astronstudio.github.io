STEP

1

Prepare your iPhone, watch, and headset. (You can also use iPhone speakers)

2

Refer to the manual of the watch to check whether the "Home City Setting" is correct. Taking "China" as an example, the watch second hand should select "HONG KONG".

3

Connect the headset and Select "BPC" in WatchWave.

Click "START", turn the volume to the maximum, and the headset will make a "Da Da Da Da ... " sound.

4

Refer to the manual of the watch, set the watch to "Perform manual receive" mode, the watch second hand should select "R".

5

Put the headset on the watch glass.

6

Waiting for the watch to receive radio waves, the watch second hand should select "W".

7

After about 3 minutes, the reception is completed and the watch second hand should select "Y".

8

End wave, check watch time.
