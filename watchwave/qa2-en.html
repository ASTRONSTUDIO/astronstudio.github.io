Q:

What to do if it fails?

A:

Refer to the manual and check the watch's reception settings are turned on.

Refer to the manual to check the home city setting and signal selection match.

Operate in a quiet environment.

When receiving, please keep away from interference items such as household appliances and office machinery.

Turn on “Do Not Disturb” Mode for your iPhone or iPad. Notification, Ringer and Alerts will interrupt the signal.

Adjust the position of the watch or headphones and try multiple times.

*If your manual is missing, please contact us in "Settings", we provide free search service.
